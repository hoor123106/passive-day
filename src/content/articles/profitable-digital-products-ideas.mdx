3\. **Apply Presets:** Now that your presets are imported, it's time to start using them!

Absolutely! Selling digital products can be highly profitable. Since there are no physical production costs or shipping fees, the profit margins tend to be higher compared to physical products. Plus, with the increasing demand for digital content, there is a vast market waiting to be tapped into.

**Is selling digital products a good side hustle?**

Definitely! Selling digital products as a side hustle offers flexibility and potential for extra income. You can create and sell them in your spare time, reaching customers worldwide. It's like having your own online store that operates 24/7 while you focus on other commitments.
